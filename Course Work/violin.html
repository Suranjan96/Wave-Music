Violin

Violins can be considered as a musical masterpiece of the ancient civilizations. The modern violin we see today is an evolved version of them.

The modern violin has been using for around 500 years. It's belived to be designed in the 1500’s by Andrea Amati.

Playing violins burn approximately 170 calories per hour. Forget about your workout and start practicing!

Violins are very complex. Over 70 different pieces of wood are put together to form the modern violin.

Violins are consisted of 4 strings which are rubbed with a using a specially designed bow to create a vibration. This vibration gets amplified in the hollow chamber underneath the strings and cause the sounds.
